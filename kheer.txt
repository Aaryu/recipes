Put pan on a stove and 1 tsp butter and 1 cup vermicellie, roast it till they turn brown. Take out the pan from stove to let cool down. 

Take a small vessel and add a 1tstp butter after the butter melts add cardomon, chopped almonds, cashews and dried grapes. Let them roast till light brown and till the dried grapes puff up. Now add 2 cups of milk and 1 cup of sugar to the roasted cashew, almonds and dried grapes. Bring the milk to boil and add the rosted vermicellie. Let it cook for 10 min. Switch off the stove and serve it hot or chilled.
